Use the `get_briefing` tool to generate a comprehensive briefing.

The briefing covers:
- Major tech and business trends from the past 14 days
- Market movements (crypto, stocks, forex)
- Cross-source confirmed signals
- Predictions and money flow analysis

If the user specifies a number of days (e.g. "briefing for last 7 days"), pass that as the days parameter.
