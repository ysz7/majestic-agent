Use the `get_briefing` tool with focus on the predictions section, or call `run_research` first if no recent data exists.

Generate predictions covering:
- Short-term (1-4 weeks): likely market and tech movements
- Medium-term (1-3 months): emerging trends reaching mainstream
- Money flows: where capital is moving based on current signals

Base all predictions on confirmed cross-source signals, not speculation.
